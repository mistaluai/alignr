# Alignr

> 🚧 **Status: Under Active Development** 🚧

Alignr is an AI-powered platform that helps anyone turn a raw app idea into a fully planned, ready-to-build software project. Through a simple, step-by-step process, a team of specialized AI assistants interviews you to understand your business goals, outlines the necessary app features, and generates live visual mockups of what your application will look like. Once you approve the design and project plan, Alignr automatically translates your vision into a detailed, step-by-step instruction manual that other AI coding tools can easily follow to build your app from scratch.
